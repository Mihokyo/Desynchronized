This is the v1.6.2 version of Desynchronized: Tales and News. Numerous attempts were made to contact Randy Random in response to the recent meltdown of the entire mod.

# Changelog
- Various improvements to the existing tale-news interface
  - Individual pawns: forgotten news are always hidden
  - Global overview: option to hide permanently-forgotten news
  - Added "forgotten news count" column
  - Added icons to replace long header texts
  - News tracker now closes when clicked outside
  - News tracker no longer shows unneccessary scroller when there aren't many tale-news
  - You may now move the camera around even when news tracker is open
  - Slight adjustment to the text alignment
- Optimized some codes
  - Standardized and greatly simplified code related to calculating news importance score
  - Reduced number of loops used when determining which tale-news to forget
  - Tale-news references now gets its subject pawn during the post-load init phase
- Fixed some bugs
  - Fixed out-of-bounds error when a pawn who does not remember any news attempts to transmit a news
- Made offerings to the One True God of RimWorld, Randy Random, to quell the spirits

#23, #25, and #26 now requires intense observation. I hope there are no bugs this time around. Afterall, Randy is hard to please.
